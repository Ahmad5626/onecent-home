Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: [Insert Date]

At OneCentHomes, we aim to provide a seamless experience for all our users. However, we understand that there may be instances where you may need to cancel a service or request a refund. This Cancellation & Refund Policy outlines the terms under which cancellations and refunds may be issued for services provided through our website.

1. Cancellation of Services

You may cancel any service or subscription offered by OneCentHomes by contacting our support team or through your account settings. Cancellations must be made prior to the renewal date of the service to avoid any charges for the upcoming billing period.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

  • If the service purchased is not provided as described.
  • If you experience technical issues that cannot be resolved by our team.
  • If a duplicate payment is made in error.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team with the details of your transaction. Refunds will be processed to the original method of payment within 7-10 business days upon approval.

4. Non-Refundable Services

Certain services or products may be non-refundable. These will be clearly marked at the time of purchase, and by proceeding with the purchase, you agree that the service is non-refundable.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Cancellation & Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly. Please review this policy periodically for updates.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us at contact@onecenthomes.com.
