Project Highlights

Imagine a world where luxury living meets eco-consciousness. Oorja Project's meticulously designed homes offer just that. Step into a future of sustainable living, where comfort and style blend seamlessly with nature.

Choose from spacious 2BHK and 3BHK apartments, each equipped with smart living features. Experience the convenience of technology integrated into your daily life, while enjoying the tranquility of your surroundings. The sleek, contemporary design, complemented by natural materials and energy-efficient systems, creates a harmonious balance of aesthetics and sustainability.

Your safety and well-being are paramount. Oorja Project offers a secure gated community with 24x7 security, wide CC roads, and smart surveillance systems. Enjoy leisurely strolls through landscaped gardens and jogging paths, while your children play safely in the dedicated play area.

Live connected to everything that matters. Oorja Project is surrounded by top-tier amenities, schools, hospitals, shopping centers, and public transport links, ensuring a convenient and hassle-free lifestyle for all residents.

Project Specifications

Structure

  • RCC frame structure using readymade concrete reinforced with high-strength steel.
  • Solid concrete blocks of 6” & 4” thick for masonry to ensure durability and stability.

Plastering

  • All internal walls are plastered neatly and finished with smooth lime rendering.
  • All external walls are plastered with cement-based plaster, providing a durable finish.

Flooring

  • Vitrified tile flooring across the entire apartment with skirting of 4”.
  • Rectified tile flooring in common areas like lobbies, corridors, and entranceways.
  • Premium granite flooring for all stairs and passageways.

Kitchen

  • Polished granite countertop with a single stainless steel sink.
  • Ceramic tile dado up to 2’ height around the kitchen platform.
  • Non-slip ceramic tiles for kitchen and utility areas.

Doors And Windows

  • Main Door: High-quality teak wood frame with a designer shutter, polished and fitted with high-grade locks.
  • Internal Doors: Engineered wooden doors with polished laminate finish.
  • Windows: UPVC windows with mosquito mesh and safety grills.

Electrical

  • Wiring: Concealed copper wiring with premium modular switches.
  • Power Backup: 100% power backup for common areas and lifts; provision for inverters inside apartments.
  • Air Conditioning: Provision for split AC units in living room and all bedrooms.

Sanitary and Plumbing

  • Sanitary Fittings: Premium ceramic sanitary fittings from reputed brands like Kohler or Jaguar.
  • Plumbing: CPVC piping for water supply, ensuring durability and non-corrosive performance.
  • Water Supply: 24-hour water supply with overhead and underground tanks.

Painting

  • Interior: Smooth finish with premium acrylic emulsion paint.
  • Exterior: Weather-resistant paint for all external walls ensuring long-term protection against the elements.

Lifts

  • High-speed automatic lifts from reputed brands with intercom facility and 24/7 power backup.

Security

  • Wiring: Concealed copper wiring with premium modular switches.
  • Power Backup: 100% power backup for common areas and lifts; provision for inverters inside apartments.
  • Air Conditioning: Provision for split AC units in living room and all bedrooms.

Water Management

  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance in all common areas.
  • Video door phone and intercom facility for each apartment.
  • Gated community with a security checkpoint at the main entrance.

Fire Safety

  • Advanced fire safety systems installed, including fire extinguishers, fire alarms, and hydrant systems.
